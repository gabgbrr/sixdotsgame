Blindness shouldn't hinder learning.

Through our product, we have created a more accessible and easy way of arithmetic learning for the visually impaired.

Say hello to Six Dots

Six Dots is an inclusive and tactile math card game specifically designed to help visually impaired children build and practice arithmetic skills in an engaging, interactive way.


Watch how it all works


Impairments are no longer a hurdle.

Unlike traditional educational board games that rely heavily on visual elements, this product integrates braille text and customized indents to ensure that players with visual impairment can participate.



Our Mission

To empower visually impaired children by developing accessible, tactile learning solutions that bridge the gap in math education. We are committed to:


Innovation

Designing durable, multi-sensory card games that utilize Braille and unique tactile indents for independent play.

Inclusivity

Creating shared environments where visually impaired and sighted learners can collaborate and compete on an equal playing field.

Engagement

Transforming arithmetic practice from a challenging obstacle into a motivating and interactive experience.
