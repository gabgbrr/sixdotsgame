Mechanics and Blueprints behind Six Dots

Mechanics

Six Dots is played using 45 tactile cards: 9 point cards and 36 math cards. Players use braille to identify the cards in their hand and on the board.


Here is how you play the game:

  • Objective: The goal is to rapidly find and combine two math cards whose sum matches the point card.

  • Gameplay: The player must place the two math cards in the correct slot of the point card. If the sum is correct, the player earns the point card (1 point).

  • Verification: Ideally, a guardian is present to check the results, but players may double-check the values themselves at the end of the round.

  • Winning: The game continues until all slots are filled. The player with the most points wins. This makes learning addition fast, fun, and competitive!
